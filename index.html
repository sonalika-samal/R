Positivity

Life in my eyes...

Life.. What is life? It seems a roller coaster to me.. Rollercoaster of emotions.. Rollercoaster of experiences.. I can also interprete life as an examination or competition that includes a range of module tests.. Where we win.. we pass.. also there is loss and we fail.. But we learn from our previous mistakes and implement that learning in our next module test of life.. I know it's easy to say but we really never learn from our previous mistakes sincerely and act as per our emotional side of mind guides us to do.. So we again face a loss in our present module test and spit out all our anger and grief on ourselves and the whole world around us, with a broken heart.. tired soul.. fed up mind.. We start seeing the whole world in the perspective of our last bunch of awful experiences.. And eventually with so many confusions, doubts and fear, built up in our mind, we become cold.. our heart becomes cold.. We start focusing on ourselves only.. The world starts judging us as selfish ones but we are okay with this because we don't expect anything great from that world anymore.. This is a phase, we learn most out of the life.. This becomes the serious preparation period for our final module test to enter a great phase of life..ðŸ˜Š

Good Vibes

But I know we sometimes become unable to act as per our serious preparation plans made with a strong mind earlier.. That state of mind sometimes at some phases of life, starts to melt and we become weak.. We start missing those beautiful memories, goals and persons for which we had come to that extend where we faced loss.. We miss those which are the reasons behind our current state of mind and soul.. How funny is it.. But I believe and am gonna write here that, we miss the previous versions of those persons and goals that had attracted and cherished us once.. fulfilled our soul once.. But as of now we have already seen their real faces or changed versions.. we should avoid all kind of feelings or emotions or that 'yess I could have done this if I would have done that' type regrets related to those past modules of life.. Because whether with failure or win, we have already overcome all those phases and yes life is all about 'moving on' and 'letting things go'.. Every person.. Every phase.. Every desire.. come and reside with us for a certain period of time.. play their roles in our life.. help us living through certain hard phases of our life.. make memories.. and leave.. Now this word 'leave' may hurt some of us.. or I can say almost everyone of us because that's the main reason behind all these plot twists of our story of life we had to go through.. But I say let's take this word 'leave' as normal as the word 'come' as I used in the first half of that sentence.. Let's take it as a flow.. or better we can say a cycle of phases that starts with 'come' and ends with 'leave'.. And again starts with 'come' and the cycle repeats.. If we want to lead to a better life for is then we should repeat the cycle in a forward manner.. not in a static manner that revolves around the same entities..
